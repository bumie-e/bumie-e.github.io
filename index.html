About

I'm an AI Engineer specialising in NLP, ads analytics, statistical modelling, and responsible AI. Currently I work as AI Engineer at Bfree Africa, building intelligent systems for financial services, and as Adjunct Faculty at Pan-Atlantic University. In March 2026, I begin a research internship at LiGHT, EPFL in Switzerland — and I've been shortlisted for the prestigious ELLIS PhD Programme, one of Europe's most competitive doctoral tracks in AI.

My research in reinforcement learning explores how agents learn to collaborate in mixed-motive environments — theory, ethics, and real-world deployment in one question. I've spoken at AMLD Africa, TEDx, Python Summit Warsaw, and ML Lagos, and I review papers for ICLR and ECCV workshops.

Being introverted has shaped how deliberately I communicate and how deeply I think. Quiet confidence, consequential work.
